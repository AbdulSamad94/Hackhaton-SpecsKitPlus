# Hardware Setup: Workstations & Robots

## Workstation Requirements

To run Isaac Sim and train VLA models, you need a powerful workstation.

-   **GPU**: NVIDIA RTX 3080 or higher (RTX 4090 recommended).
-   **CPU**: AMD Ryzen 9 or Intel Core i9.
-   **RAM**: 64GB+.
-   **Storage**: 1TB NVMe SSD.

## Humanoid Robot Platforms

### 1. Unitree H1
-   **Cost**: ~$90k
-   **Actuators**: High-torque joint motors.
-   **Sensors**: 3D LiDAR, Depth Camera.

### 2. Custom Builds (Open Source)
-   Projects like **Open Dynamic Robot Initiative**.
-   Lower cost but requires significant assembly and tuning.

## Onboard Compute

The robot itself needs a brain.
-   **NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin**: The standard for edge AI in robotics. Delivers up to 275 TOPS.

## Networking

Ensure a robust Wi-Fi 6 router or 5G connection for low-latency telemetry.
